Bring Together Vietnamese Society at Oxford.

ABOUT US

Vietnamese Society in Oxford (VOX) was founded by the University of Oxford’s Centre for Vietnamese Community and Innovation in 2013.

Society members are current undergraduate and post-graduate students at the University of Oxford, Oxford Brookes University, MBAs, EMAs, active alumni, industry experts, and thought leaders pioneering research and innovation within academia and beyond. Vietnamese Society at Oxford provides a central platform to bring together capabilities and resources from these groups to empower the Vietnamese culture, embedded in its unique position within Oxford Society.

Changing the world is possible. We’ve done it before.

Our leadership team bring years of experience to bear on the greatest challenge of our time. We’re results driven, with a proven record of previous successes.
