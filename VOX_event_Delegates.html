About the event

Linacre College, Oxford - OX3 9BX

The Vietnamese student and researcher community in Oxford had the privilege of meeting with delegates from VinUniversity, including the university's principals and the Director of VinEducation. This meeting served as a platform for exchanging ideas on educational collaboration, academic excellence, and research innovation.

The discussion highlighted opportunities for Oxford students and researchers to engage with VinUniversity's forward-thinking initiatives in Vietnam, particularly in areas of science, technology, and education. The visit not only strengthened ties between the two institutions but also opened doors for future partnerships, scholarships, and knowledge-sharing programs, enhancing the global impact of both communities.
